Best Kitchen Gadgets Worth Buying in 2024

Globora Editorial Team January 25, 2025 Product Reviews Reading Time: 15 minutes

In today's fast-paced modern life, choosing the right kitchen appliances not only improves cooking efficiency but also lets us enjoy the pleasure of cooking. This article carefully selects 15 of the most worthwhile kitchen gadgets to buy in 2024, covering comprehensive recommendations from basic tools to high-tech equipment, helping you create the ideal cooking space.

Why Do You Need to Upgrade Kitchen Equipment?

The development of modern kitchen equipment is not just about functional improvements, but more about enhancing quality of life. A set of quality kitchen equipment can:

  • Significantly save cooking time and improve efficiency
  • Ensure optimal nutrition and taste of food
  • Simplify complex cooking processes
  • Make cooking an enjoyment rather than a burden

2024 Kitchen Gadget Recommendation List

1. Multi-Function High-Speed Blender

Recommended Model: Vitamix Ascent A3500

Price Range: $500 - $650

Core Functions:

  • Super powerful blending capability, easily makes various fruit and vegetable juices
  • Smart program settings, one-touch preparation of soy milk, juices, smoothies
  • Self-cleaning function, completes cleaning in 30 seconds
  • 10-year warranty, long service life
Real test experience: Making carrot juice requires no filtering, smooth texture; excellent results with nuts and similar ingredients, a true household beverage-making gadget.

2. Air Fryer

Recommended Model: Philips HD9650/91

Price Range: $170 - $260

Core Functions:

  • 360-degree hot air circulation, low-oil healthy cooking
  • Temperature range 40-200°C, timer up to 60 minutes
  • Non-stick coated interior, easy to clean
  • 7 preset programs, suitable for different ingredients

3. Steam-Oven Combo

Recommended Model: ROBAM CQ975

Price Range: $550 - $750

Core Functions:

  • Multiple functions in one: steam, bake, fry, ferment
  • Precise temperature control, ±1°C accuracy
  • Large capacity design, meets multi-person dining needs
  • Smart recipes, easy for beginners to use

4. Dishwasher

Recommended Model: Siemens SJ236I01JC

Price Range: $360 - $500

Core Functions:

  • 13 place setting capacity, suitable for 3-6 person households
  • 72°C high-temperature sterilization, more hygienic tableware
  • Variable frequency motor, quieter operation
  • Smart sensing, automatically matches washing programs

5. Smart Rice Cooker

Recommended Model: Zojirushi NP-ZF18

Price Range: $400 - $550

Core Functions:

  • Pressure IH heating technology, better rice texture
  • Multiple rice modes, adapts to different rice varieties
  • 24-hour timer function, scheduled cooking
  • Premium inner pot coating, non-stick and easy to clean

Practical Advice for Selecting Kitchen Equipment

Budget Planning

Based on different needs and budgets, we recommend:

  • Entry-level Setup ($700-$1,400): Air fryer + Multi-function blender + Rice cooker
  • Mid-level Setup ($1,400-$2,800): Add steam-oven combo + Dishwasher to entry setup
  • High-end Setup ($2,800+): Complete smart kitchen appliance set + Professional baking equipment

Key Points to Consider When Purchasing

  • Kitchen Space: Measure kitchen dimensions to ensure equipment can be properly placed
  • Electrical Safety: Check household circuit capacity, upgrade if necessary
  • Usage Frequency: Choose based on actual usage needs, avoid post-purchase idleness
  • After-sales Service: Choose brands and retailers with good after-sales service
  • Energy Efficiency: Choose energy-efficient products for long-term savings

Kitchen Equipment Maintenance Guide

Daily Cleaning

  • Clean promptly after each use to avoid grease buildup
  • Use neutral cleaners, avoid corrosive chemicals
  • Regular deep cleaning to maintain optimal equipment performance

Regular Maintenance

  • Check power cords and plugs for damage
  • Clean ventilation and heat dissipation holes
  • Replace wear parts such as seals, filters, etc.
  • Perform professional maintenance according to manual requirements

2024 Kitchen Equipment Development Trends

Observing the 2024 kitchen equipment market, we've identified several clear trends:

  • Enhanced Intelligence: More devices support APP control and voice operation
  • Multi-function Integration: One machine, multiple uses, saving kitchen space
  • Healthy Cooking: Low-oil, low-salt healthy cooking methods are favored
  • Energy Efficiency: Energy efficiency ratings become important selection criteria
  • Personalized Customization: Customized solutions based on household needs

Summary and Purchase Recommendations

Choosing the right kitchen equipment is a long-term investment and shouldn't blindly pursue low prices or excessive features. We recommend making rational choices based on your actual needs, usage habits, and budget.

Remember, the best kitchen equipment isn't necessarily the most expensive, but the most suitable for you. Before purchasing, read more reviews, compare options, and even visit physical stores for hands-on experience before deciding.

We hope this recommendation helps you create your ideal kitchen and makes cooking an enjoyable experience!
