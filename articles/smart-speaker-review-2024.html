Best Smart Speakers 2024 In-Depth Review: Xiaomi vs Apple vs Amazon

Globora Review Team May 11, 2025 Product Reviews Reading Time: 18 minutes

Smart speakers serve as crucial entry points for smart homes, with market competition being exceptionally fierce. In this review, we selected five of the most representative products for comprehensive comparison: Xiaomi AI Speaker, Apple HomePod mini, Amazon Echo, Huawei Sound X, and Tmall Genie. From audio quality to voice recognition, from smart home control to ecosystem compatibility, we provide the most professional purchasing recommendations.

Review Product Overview

This review covers the following five mainstream smart speaker products:

Tested Product List

  • Xiaomi AI Speaker Pro (2nd Generation) - Price: $59
  • Apple HomePod mini - Price: $109
  • Amazon Echo Dot (4th Generation) - Price: $49
  • Huawei Sound X - Price: $289
  • Tmall Genie X1 - Price: $43

Review Dimensions and Methods

To ensure objectivity and comprehensiveness of the review, we established the following evaluation criteria:

Audio Quality Testing (Weight 35%)

  • Frequency response curve testing
  • Subjective listening evaluation (classical, pop, rock and other music styles)
  • Volume output testing
  • Distortion measurement

Voice Recognition Accuracy (Weight 25%)

  • Recognition rate in quiet environments
  • Recognition rate in noisy environments
  • Dialect recognition capability
  • Long-distance wake-up testing

Smart Functions (Weight 25%)

  • Smart home device control capability
  • Third-party service integration
  • Personalized recommendation accuracy
  • Multi-turn conversation understanding capability

Design and Build Quality (Weight 15%)

  • Aesthetic appeal of design
  • Material and build quality
  • Size and compatibility
  • Ease of operation

Detailed Review Results

1. Xiaomi AI Speaker Pro (2nd Generation)

Overall Score: 8.2/10

Audio Quality: 7.8/10

The Xiaomi AI Speaker Pro features two 2.5-inch woofers and one 20mm tweeter, delivering excellent performance in this price range. Bass is adequate but not excessive, midrange vocals are clear, and treble extension is good. It performs exceptionally well when playing pop music.

Voice Recognition: 8.5/10

Voice recognition based on Xiao Ai is among the top tier of domestic brands, supporting continuous dialogue with recognition accuracy exceeding 95% in quiet environments. Dialect recognition capability is strong, supporting major dialects such as Sichuan and Cantonese.

Test Highlight: In a noisy kitchen environment (with range hood running), it can still accurately recognize the command "Xiao Ai, play music". The long-distance recognition capability is impressive.

Smart Functions: 8.8/10

Xiaomi's ecosystem products are abundant, capable of controlling over 5,000 smart devices. Supports Mi Home, Zigbee and other protocols, with excellent connectivity with Xiaomi phones, TVs and other devices.

Design and Build: 7.5/10

The cylindrical design is simple and elegant, with a pleasant fabric exterior. The anti-slip design at the bottom is practical, but the overall plastic feel is strong, with build quality slightly inferior to high-end products.

2. Apple HomePod mini

Overall Score: 8.8/10

Audio Quality: 9.2/10

Despite its compact size, the HomePod mini's audio performance is impressive. Apple's computational audio technology gives this small speaker audio performance that exceeds its size. Well-balanced frequencies, wide soundstage, and excellent detail reproduction.

语音识别：8.8/10

Siri的语音识别在中文环境下表现良好，但在嘈杂环境下的表现略逊于小爱同学。支持个人识别，能够区分不同家庭成员的声音。

智能功能：8.5/10

与苹果生态深度集成，支持AirPlay、接力等功能。但第三方设备支持相对有限，主要适合苹果用户。

设计做工：9.5/10

苹果一贯的高品质做工，网状织物外壳质感出众，整体设计简约而精致。

3. Amazon Echo Dot (4th Generation)

综合评分：7.9/10

音质表现：7.2/10

作为入门级产品，Echo Dot的音质中规中矩。清晰度尚可，但低频表现有限，更适合语音交互而非音乐欣赏。

语音识别：8.8/10

Alexa的语音识别技术非常成熟，即使在复杂的语言环境下也能保持较高的准确率。支持多种语言，但中文支持相对薄弱。

智能功能：8.5/10

拥有最丰富的第三方技能库，可连接数万种智能设备。但在中国市场的本土化程度不足。

Comprehensive Review Summary

Best Value: Xiaomi AI Speaker Pro

399元的价格提供了出色的音质和智能功能，特别适合小米生态用户。语音识别准确，智能家居控制能力强，是入门智能音响的首选。

Best Audio Quality: Apple HomePod mini

尽管价格较高，但HomePod mini的音质表现确实出色。如果您是苹果用户且对音质有较高要求，值得考虑。

Best Ecosystem: Huawei Sound X

与华为手机、电脑等设备的无缝连接体验出色，特别是"一碰传音"功能非常实用。

Purchase Recommendations

Selection Guide

  • 预算有限（300元以下）：天猫精灵X1，基础功能齐全
  • 平衡选择（300-500元）：小米AI音响Pro，性价比最高
  • 音质优先（500-800元）：苹果HomePod mini，音质出众
  • 旗舰体验（1500元以上）：华为Sound X，顶级音质和设计

使用场景匹配

  • 卧室使用：推荐HomePod mini，音质好且体积小巧
  • 客厅使用：推荐华为Sound X，音质强劲且外观大气
  • 厨房使用：推荐小米AI音响Pro，抗噪能力强
  • 书房办公：推荐天猫精灵，性价比高功能够用

未来发展趋势

智能音响市场在2024年呈现以下发展趋势：

  • 音质提升：更多厂商注重音质，不再只是语音交互工具
  • 可视化界面：带屏幕的智能音响成为新趋势
  • 多房间音响：全屋音频解决方案逐渐普及
  • 本地化处理：更多功能支持离线处理，保护隐私
  • 生态整合：各品牌加强与智能家居生态的整合

总结

智能音响的选择应该基于个人的实际需求和使用场景。如果您是智能家居爱好者，建议选择与您现有设备生态匹配的产品；如果您更注重音质，可以考虑价位稍高但音质出色的产品；如果是初次尝试智能音响，性价比高的入门产品就能满足大部分需求。

无论选择哪款产品，智能音响都能为您的生活带来便利和乐趣。希望这份详细的评测能够帮助您做出最适合的选择！
